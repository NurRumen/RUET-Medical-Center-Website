RUET Medical Center

Heaven's Light is Our Guide

Ruet Medical Center is an on campus medical center for the RUET students to facilitate health services

An on campus medical center provides primary and basic health care facilities to the students (residential and non-residential) free of charges. Three MBBS doctors and other staffs provide these facilities to the students. For specialized consultation on complicated cases, the center refers the patients to specialist consultants.

Services are provided
Saturday-Thursday: 07:30AM to 10:00PM, Lunch Break: 1:30PM to 3:00PM.
Friday: Off Day.

Minor Surgery - procedures are carried out under local anaesthetic in the medical centre.

treatment of illnesses and injuries
women's health care including contraception, pap smears, pregnancy testing and advice, and antenatal care
testing and counselling for sexually transmitted infections
vaccinations, including travel immunisations and advice
immunisations required for students in health related programs
skin checks
assistance with emotional and personal difficulties
mental health services including psychologists, mental health nurse and specialist psychiatrist
referral to specialists, imaging services, pathology, and physiotherapy
